About YourGuide

Empowering local guides & reshaping the future of travel

Who We Are

YourGuide is a community-driven platform that connects travelers with independent guides around the world. We believe in authentic experiences, local insights, and ensuring that guides are recognized—and compensated—fairly for their expertise.

Our mission: “Never charge guides, ever.” We aim to remove barriers for independent tour guides who want to share their passion with travelers.

Our Revenue Model

To keep the platform free for guides, we monetize in ways that don’t affect their earnings:

  • Traveler Service Fees: A small fee from travelers when they book, ensuring guides keep 100% of their listed rates.
  • Optional Memberships: Travelers can subscribe for premium features (e.g., advanced AI itineraries, priority support).
  • Local Partnerships: We partner with local businesses for sponsored content or deals. All partnerships are clearly labeled.

We are fully transparent about our fees and where they go, so everyone knows how their money is used.
