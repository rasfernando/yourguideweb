Our Core Values

What drives us every day at YourGuide

What We Stand For

These values guide every decision we make.

Transparency

We’re clear about how guides are compensated and how we earn money—no hidden fees or surprises.

Authenticity

We prioritize real human connections and highlight local culture, stories, and experiences.

Community-Centric

We foster a sense of belonging among guides and travelers, encouraging collaboration and shared experiences.

Empowerment

Guides keep 100% of what they earn. We remove barriers so everyone can thrive.

Inclusivity & Accessibility

We welcome guides and travelers of all backgrounds and abilities—travel is for everyone.

Local Impact & Sustainability

We encourage responsible, small-scale tourism that benefits local communities and the environment.
