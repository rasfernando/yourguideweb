YourGuide Account & Data Deletion

Effective Date: 13 January 2025

1. Introduction

At YourGuide, we believe it is important for users to have full control over their personal data. This page outlines how you can request the deletion of your account and any associated data. Please note that our app does not currently support in-app deletion; all deletion requests must be made via email.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to admin@yourguide.city with the subject line: “Account Deletion Request”.

In your email, please include:

  • Your full name (as used in the YourGuide app)
  • The email address associated with your YourGuide account
  • A brief statement confirming that you wish to delete your account

Note: We may require additional information to verify ownership of the account before proceeding with the deletion.

3. Data That Will Be Deleted

Once we have verified your request, we will delete:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and any profile details you provided.
  • Usage data associated with your account, which may include your location history (if permission was granted) and any preferences you have saved.
  • Purchase history tied to your account (although anonymized transaction records may be retained by our payment processor for legal/audit purposes).

4. Data Retention

Certain types of information may need to be retained for a limited period to fulfill legal or administrative obligations, such as record-keeping and fraud prevention. In such cases, this data is securely stored and inaccessible for any other use. We will permanently delete your account information once all legal retention requirements have been met.

5. Deletion Timeline

We strive to process all verified account deletion requests within 30 days of confirmation. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted from our systems.

6. Irreversible Action

Please note that account and data deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. If you wish to use YourGuide again, you will need to create a new account.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our account deletion process or our data practices, please email us at admin@yourguide.city.

Thank you for using YourGuide. We value your privacy and are committed to making our data deletion process as clear and straightforward as possible.
